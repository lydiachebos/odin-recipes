Delicious Pancakes Recipe

Description

Pancakes are a popular breakfast dish that can be served with variety of toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk(adjust for consistency)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract(optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg, then add milk, melted butter and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients and gently stir until you obtain a smooth batter.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it. Pour about 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface(about 2 minutes), then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Stack and serve warm with syrup, butter, fruits or any toppings of choice.
