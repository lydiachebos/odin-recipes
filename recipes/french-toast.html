How to Prepare French Toast

Description

A classic breakfast dish made from bread slices. It is soft and slightly crispy

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract(optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon(optional)
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • syrup or honey or fruits

Steps

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, vanilla and cinnamon.
  2. Dip each bread slice into the mixture coating both sides evenly.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat and cook each slice for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown.
  4. Serve warm with syrup, honey or fruits
